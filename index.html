FSM Rules:

  • Start: Initial state
  • State 1: After first black ball
  • State 2: After white follows black
  • State 3: After black follows white (with overlap OFF)
  • Accept: Pattern "black, white, black" detected

With overlap ON, the sequence can reuse balls for multiple patterns.
